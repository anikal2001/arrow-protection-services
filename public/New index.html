The Three Mottos



Dependability

Arrow Protection Services has been a reliable Security Company in the GTA since 2006. Being a great service provider means having total confidence in the people providing these services. We’re proud to have the best pros in the business working with us. With a focus on personalized service, competitive rates, and customer satisfaction, we’re always striving to exceed our clients’ expectations.

Proffessionalism

At Arrow Protection Services, we hold ourselves to the highest standards with the services we offer, and in every aspect of the work we do. Our workforce is highly trained and qualified to meet all your needs, dedicated to offering diverse and specialized services tailored to our client’s unique preferences and situation. Take a look at some of the services we offer below.

Commitment

APS management team realizes that one of the differences in our security personnel and that of our competitors is our commitment to training. Our provincial licensed instructors teach from one of the most comprehensive training programs. We utilize cutting edge computers and video training techniques.
